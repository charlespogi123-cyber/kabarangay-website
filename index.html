Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a Barangay Clearance?

You can request a Barangay Clearance by visiting the barangay hall during office hours. Bring a valid ID and proof of residence. You can also request online through our Services page.

What are the requirements for a Business Permit?

Requirements for a Business Permit include: Barangay Clearance, Mayor's Permit, DTI or SEC Registration, Lease Contract or Proof of Building Ownership, and Sketch of Business Location.

How can I report a community concern?

You can report community concerns by visiting the barangay hall, calling our office at (02) 123-4567, or sending an email to info@barangay.com. For emergencies, please call the barangay hotline.

What are the office hours of the Barangay Hall?

The Barangay Hall is open from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and holidays.

How can I participate in barangay activities?

Check our Announcements page regularly for upcoming activities. You can also visit the barangay hall to inquire about ongoing programs and how to participate.

How do I get a Certificate of Indigency?

To get a Certificate of Indigency, visit the barangay hall with valid IDs, proof of income, and proof of residence. The barangay social worker will assist you with the process.
